ABOUT MYSELF

As a dedicated youth life coach and CEO of Ulralife Project, a youth development initiative, I specialize in empowering young individuals to overcome challenges and achieve their full potential. With a background in psychology and extensive experience in mentoring, I provide personalized guidance on academic success, career planning, and personal development. My approach combines evidence-based techniques with empathy and understanding, creating a supportive environment where youth can thrive. Passionate about fostering resilience and self-confidence, I am committed to helping young people navigate life's complexities and build a foundation for a successful and fulfilling future.
